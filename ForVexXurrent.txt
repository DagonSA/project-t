Tile

120×140px, pointy-top hex
Usable offset radius: ~±50px horizontal, ~±55px vertical
Current solo formation uses offsets relative to tile world center: [Vector2(0,-30), Vector2(-30,10), Vector2(30,10)]

Formation rules

Standoff axis: left vs right (vertical dividing line through tile center)
Team A → left side (x negative), Team B → right side (x positive)
Same team stacks vertically on their side
Solo char on a side → centered at that side's anchor (y=0)
Two chars [Vector2(-20, 0), Vector2(20, 0)]
Three chars [Vector2(0, -47), Vector2(-20, 23), Vector2(20, 23)]
Solo overall (1v0) → dead center (0,0)
Side x anchors: ±28px (conservative, well within tile)
Monsters treated as a team, same logic applies; 1v1v1 deferred

Formations needed (player combos only)

1v0, 2v0, 3v0 (solo team)
1v1, 2v1, 1v2, 2v2, 3v1, 1v3, 3v2, 2v3, 3v3
